BACKGROUND

NutriBullet Balance helps you build the perfect smoothie based on your nutrition goals and preferences. While working with Mutual Mobile, I had the opportunity to work with the design and engineering team at NutriBullet to create visual designs for the balance app.

HOW IT WORKS

CONNECT
Open the app and then connect the blender to your phone. The blue light at the bottom of the blender will indicate that the device is successfully connected.

SET PREFERENCES
Set dietary preferences based on nutrition goals, likes and dislikes and wellness goals.

BROWSE RECIPES
Browse through recipes that match your particular needs. You can see nutrition information about each recipe and details of all ingredients. You can also customize a recipe to create your own.

BLEND
The app gives you a step by step process for making the recipe with the nutrition data getting updated real time as you add each ingredient in the blender

TRACK
After consuming the smoothie, NutriBullet weighs the amount consumed and tracks nutritional information. This inforramtion is available on the dashboard
