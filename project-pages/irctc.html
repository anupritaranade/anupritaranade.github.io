BACKGROUND

Indian Railway Catering and Tourism Corporation (IRCTC) handles online booking of train tickets, catering and tourism services for the Indian Railways. Around 5,50,000 to 6,00,000 bookings happen everyday. IRCTC Rail Connect is an Android app that aims to make the ticket booking process faster and easier.

GOAL

The goal of the project was to evaluate the existing android ticket booking app and provide suggestions to enhance user experience for hassle free ticket booking

RESEARCH

I interviewed users and tested the usability of the existing app by making them perform a ticket booking task. This process helped me gain insights about various user groups and how they interact with the app. Here are two sample vidoes of the usability testing:

HEURISTIC ANALYSIS

Following my user research, I conducted a Heuristic analysis of the app. This helped me figure out the reasons behind the identified pain points. I assigned a severity rating to each of the identified pain points.

I used the following heuristics to evaluate the usability of the app:

#user control and freedom #error prevention #flexibility and efficiency of use #aesthetic and minimal design #recognition rather than recall

NEW BOOKING

1. It is difficult to understand where exactly to tap to enter the FROM and TO fields.

#recognition rather than recall

2. All the input fields have been crammed into a tiny space while leaving a lot of space unused at the bottom

#flexibility and efficiency of use #aesthetic and minimal design

FEATURE INTRODUCTION

1. The screen provides too much information at one time and this makes it diffuclt for users to understand, defying the very purpose of this screen (to educate users about the app)

#aesthetic and minimal design

2. Most users who participated in the usability test tried tapping on the information because It also mislead them to think that the information is interactive and they can tap on it.

#flexibility and efficiency of use

3. The main call to action “Got it” gets lost in the busy layout

#aesthetic and minimal design #flexibility and efficiency of use

TRAIN LIST

1. “Departure”, “Arrival” and “Travel Time” filters took a lot of users quite some time to understand.

#recognition rather than recall

2. Users have to go back one step to edit their search critera and view results.

#flexibility and efficiency of use #error prevention #user control and freedom

3.The more option gives information about train route. Many users tapped on the more option and got lost. They couldn’t return to the train list screen.

#flexibility and efficiency of use

ADDING PASSENGERS

1. App bar on the top is cluttered with visual elements and call to actions which most users can’t recognize. The IRCTC and CRIS logos on the top look like buttons. Most users tried to tap on them.

#aesthetic and minimal design #recognition rather than recall

2. None of the users understood the function of the “Preferences” button as it is inappropriately placed with the “Add Passenger” and “Add Child” buttons

#recognition rather than recall

3. All the input fields have been crammed into a tiny space while leaving a lot of space unused at the bottom

#aesthetic and minimal design

PAYMENT

1. Categorization is leading to confusion. Example: PayTM wallet would be better placed in ‘e-Wallets’ option.

#recognition rather than recall

2. Number of taps required can be optimized by spreading out the options in the same screen.

#flexibility and efficiency of use

3. All payment options have been crammed up into a tiny space while leaving a lot of space unused at the bottom.

#aesthetic and minimal design

BRAINSTORMING

DEFINING THE INFORMATION ARCHITECTURE

I resolved to using a bottom navigation where all the content of the application is available upfront and arranged based on the priority of the functionality.

Ticket booking being the core functionality of the app, is placed at the center.
Many users check their ticket’s PNR status and visit previous bookings for cancellation.
The profile and other options are seldom used

WIREFRAMES

LOGIN

1. Easy login access with PIN.
2. Other loging option (username and password) is just one tap away in case user forgets PIN
3. ‘Book Meal’, ‘Air Ticket’, and ‘IRCTC Tourism’ are lesser used features of the app. They are displayed as suggestions with PlayStore links.

FIND TRAINS

1. Simplified interaction: Demarcates Station input and Journey Date.
2. Input fields for Stations are appropriately highlighted for user input along with the option to swap
3. Journey date selection displayed in a separate section, and the options have same prominence to avoid confusion

ADD PASSENGERS

1. User can view passenger list as she updates each passenger’s details
2. Easy to edit Boarding Point and Phone Number
3. The add passenger form opens in the same screen

PAYMENT

1. Categorised the payment methods as per the appropriate category
2. Expanded views have list views so any one option can be selected on the same screen
