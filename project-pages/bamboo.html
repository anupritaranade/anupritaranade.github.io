BACKGROUND

As a group of 15 people, we built a bamboo structure to explore the structural properties and usage of bamboo as a building material. The goal was to build a space for the university campus where students could hang out.

INITIAL IDEAS

We built scaled down models of various structures to visualise the aesthetic appeal and technical feasibility.
